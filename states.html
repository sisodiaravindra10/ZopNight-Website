§ 14 · state patterns

Every state has a pattern.

404 is the one people know. But 500, 403, offline, maintenance, empty, loading, progress all follow the same grammar. Huge figure or icon · mono status meta · one declarative headline · one sentence of plain copy · one primary + one secondary action. Never apologetic, never panicked, always a way out.

§ 01 · 404 · shipped

The canonical error layout.

Live at /404.html, huge orange figure with blue offset block, status meta, 2-line headline, plain-English body, primary + secondary CTA, quicklink chips, and a playable Tetris mini-game. Every other error state inherits this shape, scaled down.

Three kinds of empty.

No-data (first-time, nothing exists yet) · No-results (filtered or searched, nothing matches) · No-permission (exists but you can't see it). Each has its own icon and call-to-action.

§ 04 · loading

Skeleton rows + progress bar.

The skeleton shimmer uses a moving orange-tint band (our only sanctioned linear-gradient outside the homepage wash) to signal liveness. Progress bar is solid orange fill on a dark track with the same shimmer. Never a spinning wheel, motion belongs to the piece, not the spinner.

§ 05 · rules

Do · Don’t.

DO
  • Lead with a huge figure / glyph / icon — the status is visual first
  • State the status in plain mono meta (“server error” · “offline”)
  • Two sentences max in the body — declarative, never apologetic
  • One primary + one secondary action — always a way out
  • Keep the live-ticker ABOVE the state (reassures the rest works)
  • Use blue #4A66D4 for the offset block accent on error figures
  • Skeleton shimmer for < 2s loads · progress bar for > 2s
DON'T
  • No “Oops!” · no “Something went wrong” · no apologies
  • No spinning wheels · no indeterminate loaders without context
  • No stock illustrations of broken robots or sad faces
  • No dead ends — every error page has a button back
  • No buried error codes — surface the number (500, 403)
  • No skeleton rows that don’t match the loading content’s shape
  • No more than one shimmer animation on-screen at once
