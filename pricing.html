04 · faq

Common pricing questions.

If you don't see your question here, talk to our team, we'll give you a straight answer.

Do I pay per user? +

No. ZopNight pricing is per cloud account, one account means one AWS account, one Azure subscription, or one GCP project. You can add your entire engineering organization, your FinOps team, your CFO, and your SREs to a single workspace with no additional cost.

We made this decision deliberately: seat-based pricing penalizes collaboration. When FinOps tools cost per user, teams limit access to protect budget. That creates information silos, exactly the opposite of what you need when you're trying to right-size cloud spend across organizations.

What counts as a cloud account? +

One connected account in ZopNight corresponds to one cloud provider account:

AWS: One AWS Account ID (a 12-digit number like 123456789012).
Azure: One Azure Subscription (identified by its Subscription ID).
GCP: One GCP Project (identified by its Project ID).

AWS Organizations management accounts and member accounts each count separately. If you have an AWS Organization with 20 member accounts, that's 20 connected accounts. We offer volume pricing for large account portfolios, contact us before you connect more than 10.

Can I self-host on any plan? +

Self-hosting is an Enterprise-only feature. It requires infrastructure setup, ongoing maintenance, and a dedicated deployment support engagement, which is why it comes with an Enterprise CSM.

Self-hosted ZopNight runs in your VPC. Your cloud credentials never leave your network. We provide a container image, Helm chart, and Terraform module. Updates are pushed to your registry on your schedule. If data residency, air-gapped environments, or regulated infrastructure are requirements, self-hosting is the right path, talk to our team about timelines and requirements.

Is there a free trial for Pro? +

Yes, 14 days, no credit card required. When you sign up for a Pro trial, you get full access to every Pro feature: unlimited accounts, 90-day history, unlimited remediations, Slack/PagerDuty, MCP Server, RBAC, and priority support.

At the end of the trial, you can convert to paid or drop back to the Starter plan (2 accounts, 14-day history). We don't auto-charge at trial end. A human from our team will reach out before expiration to help you evaluate fit, not to pressure you.

How does Marketplace billing work? +

ZopNight is available on AWS Marketplace, Azure Marketplace, and GCP Marketplace for Enterprise customers. Marketplace billing means your ZopNight subscription appears on your existing cloud invoice and counts toward your cloud provider commitment spend (EDP, MACC, or CUD depending on provider).

This matters for teams who have committed spending agreements with AWS, Azure, or GCP, Marketplace purchases draw down those commitments, effectively making ZopNight funded by your existing cloud budget rather than a separate line item requiring separate procurement approval.

To set up Marketplace billing, contact your account executive or request a call. The process typically takes 3–5 business days.
